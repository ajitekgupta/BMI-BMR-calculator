Understanding Your Metrics

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) uses your height and weight to estimate if your weight is in a healthy range. It is a quick screening tool, though it doesn't measure body fat directly.

What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the baseline number of calories your body needs to function at rest (like breathing and blood circulation). We multiply this by your activity level to get your daily caloric needs.
